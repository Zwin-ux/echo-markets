# Design System - Echo Markets

## Direction
Echo Markets should feel like a real trading and simulation console with a culture-market layer. It can be playful in product concept, but the UI should be disciplined, legible, and enterprise-grade.

## Visual Rules
- Use dark neutral panels, quiet dividers, and one restrained blue accent.
- Use green, red, and amber only for market/status meaning.
- Avoid neon terminal styling, glitch effects, cyan everywhere, gradient buttons, emoji-led sections, and bubbly card stacks.
- Shared primitives should stay flat: 3px radius, no decorative shadows, compact headers.
- Icons are utility markers. Do not use icon-heavy decoration to fill space.

## Layout Rules
- Prioritize market-console hierarchy: header status, tabs, narrative loop, market table, trade console, feed.
- Prefer rows, ledgers, stats, and compact panels over feature-card grids.
- Copy should describe the simulation state and player action, not hype the product.

## Current Custom SVG
- `public/echo-mark.svg` is the product mark: framed market window, price pulse, and central signal block.
